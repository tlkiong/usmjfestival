ABOUT

The vision of USM Japanese Language Society is to promote Japanese culture including Japanese food, art works, and anime, traditional and modern performances. The event is organized by a bunch of students of different races and different backgrounds but is passionate and is interested in Japanese culture. Believing that there are students having the same interest and passion towards Japanese culture, USM Japanese Language society would organize the second Japan Festival in USM to meet the high demand from the students with much more improvement compared to the first.

We are here to organize this event as:

  1. to allow students to understand and learn more about Japanese culture
  2. to cultivate respect and values among the cultural differences among students
  3. to promote the relationship between the local and international students, especially Japanese students
  4. to promote unity among the students
  5. to provide a platform for the students to involve in organizing an event.
